# AtmosFlow White Paper — Target Audience and Go-to-Market

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Credentialed Industrial Hygienists (CIHs, CSPs)

**Who they are:** Board-certified professionals conducting IAQ investigations as part of their consulting practice. Typically employed by or operating IH/EHS consulting firms serving commercial, institutional, and governmental clients.

**What they need:**
- Faster report turnaround without sacrificing quality
- Consistent scoring methodology they can defend in peer review
- Standards-referenced findings with transparent benchmark classification
- Instrument chain of custody documentation
- Sampling plan generation tied to specific hypotheses

**How AtmosFlow helps:** Eliminates 8–20 hours of post-field report preparation. The assessor uses their own professional instruments, captures data through the structured walkthrough, and receives a consulting-grade report suitable for professional review — before leaving the building.

**Pricing:** Solo ($149/month, 50 credits) or Pro ($349/month, 200 credits)

### Secondary: Facility Managers and Property Managers

**Who they are:** Building operators managing one or more commercial properties. Not credentialed IH professionals. Responsible for tenant satisfaction, maintenance coordination, and building documentation.

**What they need:**
- A way to document building conditions without hiring a consultant for every complaint
- Complaint tracking that produces defensible records
- Clear escalation signals when a professional is needed
- Portfolio-level visibility across multiple properties

**How AtmosFlow helps:** FM mode provides a simplified assessment workflow using consumer or prosumer air quality monitors. The platform documents conditions, tracks complaints and interventions, and fires escalation rules when conditions exceed the facility manager's scope — routing them to a credentialed professional.

**Pricing:** Solo ($149/month, 50 credits)

### Tertiary: EHS Directors at Large Organizations

**Who they are:** Corporate EHS leaders managing safety programs across multiple facilities. May have CIH on staff or retain outside consultants.

**What they need:**
- Standardized IAQ assessment methodology across all facilities
- Portfolio-level trend data and benchmarking
- Defensible documentation for litigation and regulatory inquiries
- Integration with existing EHS management systems

**How AtmosFlow helps:** Team tier ($799/month, 500 credits) supports multi-seat deployments with standardized workflows and consolidated reporting.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta (Current)

- Free during beta with 5 credits on signup
- Direct outreach to CIH/CSP professionals via LinkedIn and AIHA/ASSP channels
- Early access waitlist at `atmosiq.prudenceehs.com/early-access`
- Feedback collection from beta users to refine scoring methodology and report format

### Phase 2: Launch

- Transition to paid subscriptions (Solo / Pro / Team)
- Stripe billing with monthly credits
- AIHA and ASSP conference presence
- Published white paper and scoring methodology documentation
- IH directory seeded with real credentialed professionals (by region)

### Phase 3: Expansion

- FM mode marketing to commercial property management firms (BOMA, IFMA channels)
- Building-type profiles expanded (healthcare, education, data center)
- Integration APIs for EHS management platforms
- Multi-product bundling with RegLens, OSHAready, HydroScan

## Competitive Moat

1. **Scoring methodology IP.** The 5-category deterministic scoring system with sufficiency checks, confidence modeling, and AIHA worst-zone override is original to Prudence EHS.

2. **Standards-aware benchmark classification.** No competing product distinguishes between OEL, REL, PHG, VSB, TCC, and ICT benchmark types in its scoring logic.

3. **Dual-mode architecture.** No competing product serves both credentialed IH professionals and facility managers with the same underlying engine.

4. **Defensibility by design.** The fail-closed scoring, evidence basis tagging, instrument chain of custody, and restrained language guardrails are architectural — not cosmetic. They cannot be replicated by adding disclaimers to a generic scoring tool.

5. **Report engine.** The 12-section consulting-grade report with scoring transparency appendix, causal pathway analysis, and hypothesis-driven sampling plan exceeds the output of any existing IAQ software.
