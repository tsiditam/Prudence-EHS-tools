## Summary

Three targeted edits were made to align the MSA with AtmosFlow's product scope. All other sections — warranties, disclaimers, indemnification, limitation of liability, data security, intellectual property, term and termination, general provisions, and Exhibit A (SLA) — were preserved unchanged.

-----

## Change 1 — Recitals, first WHEREAS clause

**Before:**

> WHEREAS, PSEC provides access to a proprietary software-as-a-service product for environmental, health, and safety management; and

**After:**

> WHEREAS, PSEC provides access to AtmosFlow, a proprietary software-as-a-service platform for the conduct of indoor air quality assessments and the generation of associated assessment reports; and

**Rationale:** Identifies AtmosFlow by name and describes its function as an indoor air quality assessment platform.

-----

## Change 2 — Section 1.9, definition of "Services"

**Before:**

> "Services" means PSEC's AtmosFlow software-as-a-service product, as identified in an Order Form.

**After:**

> "Services" means PSEC's AtmosFlow software-as-a-service product, an indoor air quality assessment platform that supports the conduct of indoor air quality surveys and the generation of associated assessment reports for review by Customer's qualified personnel, as identified in an Order Form. The Services do not provide professional opinions, regulatory determinations, certifications, or compliance assessments.

**Rationale:** Preserves the original "…as identified in an Order Form" structure and adds two sentences establishing what AtmosFlow does and the scope-of-services boundary that the Services do not provide professional opinions or compliance determinations.

-----

## Change 3 — Section 2.3, addition of subsection (g)

**Before:** Restrictions clause ended at subsection (f).

**After:** Existing (a) through (f) preserved verbatim. New (g) appended:

> …; or (g) represent to any third party that the Services or PSEC have provided a professional opinion, regulatory determination, certification, or compliance assessment.

**Rationale:** Customer obligation prohibiting misrepresentation of AtmosFlow output as a professional opinion or compliance determination. Supports the scope language in §1.9.
