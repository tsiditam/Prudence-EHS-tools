Field-to-Report Workflow

Guided Assessment. Draft Reports in Minutes.

AtmosFlow guides users through the IAQ assessment process so key building details, field observations, occupant concerns, HVAC information, photos, logger data, and investigation notes are captured in a structured workflow. Once the assessment information is collected, AtmosFlow helps transform that information into findings, charts, pathways, recommendations, and a structured draft report in minutes.

Professional review remains in control.
